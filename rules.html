Checkers Rules

  • The game is played on an 8x8 board with alternating colored tiles.
  • Each player starts with 12 pieces placed on dark squares of the first three rows.
  • Players move one piece per turn, diagonally forward to an empty square.
  • If an opponentâ€™s piece is diagonally adjacent and the square beyond it is empty, you can jump over and capture it.
  • Multiple jumps are allowed in a single turn if possible.
  • When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a "King"(Given an orangish border).
  • Kings can move and capture both forward and backward diagonally.
  • The game ends when a player has no remaining pieces or legal moves.
